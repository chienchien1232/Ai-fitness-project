# 1. Product Overview

AI Fitness Coach is an intelligent fitness assistant designed to help users improve their exercise technique and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

The application combines Computer Vision, wearable health data, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide personalized workout guidance, nutrition planning, and real-time coaching.

Unlike traditional fitness applications, AI Fitness Coach not only tracks activities but also understands user movements, evaluates exercise quality, and generates intelligent recommendations based on the user's health condition.
